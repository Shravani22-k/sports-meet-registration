📋 About the meet

Built by athletes,
for athletes.

The Annual Sports Meet started as a single college fixture and has grown into a multi-day, multi-sport festival open to students and staff from across the city.

Our story

Why we run this meet

Every August, teams and individuals gather at the City Sports Complex to compete, set personal bests, and represent their institutions. The meet is organised entirely by a student-led committee, with support from the sports department, to give athletes of every level — from first-timers to seasoned competitors — a proper stage to play on.

🎯

Our mission

Make competitive sport accessible to every student, regardless of experience level.

🤝

Fair play first

Certified officials and a published rulebook for every sport on the card.

🌱

Growing every year

Started with 3 sports in 2019 — now running 9 categories across 3 days.
